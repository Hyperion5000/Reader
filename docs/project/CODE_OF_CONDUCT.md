# Code of Conduct

Reader is a small open-source project for local document conversion. Contributors and users should keep discussions respectful, practical, and privacy-aware.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when reporting bugs or reviewing changes.
- Keep feedback focused on the project and the specific issue.
- Do not publish private documents, personal data, or sensitive case details.
- Use anonymized or synthetic examples for OCR and conversion problems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing another person's private documents or personal data.
- Sharing malware, harmful instructions, or intentionally misleading reports.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this code of conduct, and may block users who repeatedly create privacy, security, or abuse risks.
